Macaroni

Recipe description

Macaroni and cheese is a beloved comfort food that brings a sense of nostalgia with every creamy, cheesy bite. This classic dish features al dente macaroni pasta enveloped in a velvety cheese sauce, typically made from a blend of sharp cheddar, creamy Gruyère, and sometimes a hint of tangy Parmesan. The sauce, thick and luscious, clings to each piece of pasta, creating a satisfying, indulgent experience. Topped with a crunchy breadcrumb layer, it’s baked to perfection, achieving a golden-brown crust that contrasts delightfully with the gooey interior.

Beyond its simple ingredients, macaroni and cheese embodies warmth and familiarity, making it a favorite for both kids and adults alike. Often served as a side dish or a main course, it can be customized with additional ingredients like crispy bacon, sautéed vegetables, or spicy jalapeños for an extra kick. Whether enjoyed at a family gathering, a cozy night in, or as a nostalgic treat, macaroni and cheese is a dish that transcends generations, offering comfort and joy with each delicious spoonful.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) box elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Gather the ingredients.

  2. Step 2

    Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 8 minutes.

  3. Step 3

    At the same time, melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.

  4. Step 4

    Add flour, salt, and pepper and stir until smooth, about 5 minutes.

  5. Step 5

    Pour in milk slowly, while stirring continuously. Continue to cook and stir until mixture is smooth and bubbling, about 5 minutes, making sure the milk doesn't burn.

  6. Step 6

    Add Cheddar cheese and stir until melted, 2 to 4 minutes.

  7. Step 7

    Drain macaroni and fold into cheese sauce until coated.

  8. Step 8

    Serve hot and enjoy!
