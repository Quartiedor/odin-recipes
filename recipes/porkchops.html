Pork Chops

Recipe description

Oven-Fried Pork Chops are a deliciously crispy and flavorful alternative to traditional frying , offering a healthier take on a classic comfort food. The recipe begins with thick-cut pork chops that are seasoned to perfection with a blend of spices, including garlic powder, paprika, and a hint of cayenne for a subtle kick. Each chop is then coated in a mixture of breadcrumbs and panko, which ensures an extra crunchy texture when baked. This method locks in the juices, keeping the meat tender and moist while delivering that satisfying crunch that makes every bite irresistible.

Baking the pork chops in the oven not only cuts down on the oil but also allows for easy preparation, making it a perfect weeknight dinner option. Paired with sides like roasted vegetables or creamy mashed potatoes, these oven-fried chops create a comforting and balanced meal. In just about 30 minutes, you can enjoy a plate of crispy, golden-brown pork chops that will have your family asking for seconds. With this simple yet satisfying recipe, you'll discover that achieving that deep-fried flavor and texture can be both effortless and healthier!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup herb-seasoned dry bread stuffing mix
  • 4 pork chops, trimmed

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
  3. Pour butter into a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  4. Whisk together egg, milk, and pepper in a shallow dish.
  5. Place stuffing mix in a separate shallow dish. Dip pork chops in egg mixture, then coat with stuffing mix.
  6. Lay pork chops in the prepared dish.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 minutes. Flip chops and bake until no longer pink in the center and juices run clear, about 10 more minutes.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
